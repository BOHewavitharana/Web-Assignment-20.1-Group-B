Our Facilities

Sea Side South park Beach Hotel is a lovely hotel, in the heart of coastal Sri lanka. In addition to excellent transport links at the meeting point of a main metro line, our hotel is surrounded by the city's famous galle fortress, beutiful coral reefs plus many shops, cafés, restaurants, barss and other tourist sights just a few steps away. Recently, our whole hotel has undergone a complete renovation.
All 85 rooms and suits represent the popular modern yet elegant style of international boutique hotels. Each of our rooms with their simple, fresh, and warm style, and welcoming colours meet the latest city design and comfort standards. The stylish interiors of our rooms are completed by LCD television, high-speed wireless internet connection. Most of our rooms are equipped with queen-size double beds. All rooms are non-smoking.

Reception hall

Celebrate your life's special moments with our unique and luxurious styled venues with a dedicated team who will help make your speacial day a memorable one.
Our venues include Pearl Ballroom, Sapphire Ballroom, Emerald Ballroom, Diamond Ballroom.

Dinning Area

The hotel offers a rich international buffet breakfast with hot and cold food selection. The hotel’s Lounge Café and Drink Bar provides a perfect place for chilling during the day, where guests can enjoy a great selection of coffee, tea, cocktails, and other beverages.

Gym

Our hotel's comprehenssive facilities include a fully equipped state-of-the-art qymansium available 24 Hrs. We are equipped with advanced cardio machines to free weights to trained instructors committed towards your wellness.

Pool

Ideally situated in a tranquil environment, our spacious swimming pool is the best place to enjoy a dip after a day of sightseeing, or unwind with friends and family. Our rooftop pool, Jacuzzi and sunbeds are open 06:00am to 09:00pm daily for you and your family to refresh and relax.
